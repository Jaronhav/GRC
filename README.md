## Overview  
This private repository serves as a **personal reference library** of GRC-related documentation, frameworks, and policies.  
It centralizes **non-confidential** materials, notes, and references to publicly available standards (e.g., NIST, ISO, CJIS, and state cybersecurity frameworks) to support ongoing professional development and operational readiness for a Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) role.

---

## Purpose  
The goal of this repository is to:

- Maintain an organized, searchable knowledge base of **GRC frameworks, policies, and controls**  
- Reference **official publications and standards** relevant to information assurance and risk management within **state and law enforcement environments**  
- Develop and refine **internal documentation templates**, **risk assessment workflows**, and **policy examples** aligned with industry best practices  
- Serve as a living repository for continuous learning and professional growth
